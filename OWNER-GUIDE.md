# Running your Trims & Bubbles website — a plain-language guide

Hi! This is everything you need to run the website yourself from your phone or computer.
You never need to touch any code. Just sign in and use the buttons.

## Signing in

Go to **yourwebsite.com/admin** and sign in with your email and password.
That's your control panel. On a phone, tap the **menu (☰)** in the top corner to move around.

## The things you'll do most

### 📅 Set when you're open
**Menu → Availability.** Turn each day on or off and set your start/finish times.
Tap **Save**. That's your normal week.

### 🌴 Take a day off (or change one day's hours)
Same **Availability** page, under **"Days off & different hours"**: pick a date, choose
"Closed all day" or "Custom hours", and add. To undo it, tap the bin icon (it will ask you to
confirm first). *Tip: this doesn't cancel existing bookings on that day — check your calendar.*

### 🐶 See today's and this week's appointments
**Menu → Today** shows today at a glance. **Menu → Calendar** shows the week.

### ❌ Cancel or 🔄 reschedule an appointment
Open the appointment (tap **"View details"**). You can **Cancel** it or **Reschedule** it to a
new time. The customer gets an email automatically. *(Emails start working once your email
account is connected — see the note at the bottom.)*

### 💰 See who owes money / record a cash payment
**Menu → Payments** shows what's collected and what's owed. On an appointment you can record a
payment someone gave you in person. (Refunds are owner-only.)

## Your online shop

### 🛒 Add, edit, or remove a product
**Menu → Shop Products.** Tap **Add product**, fill in the name, price (in dollars), a
description, and upload a photo. Toggle **Sold out** when you run out, or **Active** off to hide
it. Changes show on your shop straight away — no waiting on anyone.

### 📦 See store orders
**Menu → Store Orders** lists what people have ordered.

## Your photo gallery

### 🖼️ Add or remove gallery photos
**Menu → Gallery.** Upload a photo with an optional caption, and it appears on your public
Gallery page. You can also flip on **"Show on public gallery"** for photos you took during an
appointment. Remove any photo with the bin icon (it confirms first).

## Your business details

### ⚙️ Change your address, phone, qualifications, social links
**Menu → Settings.** Everything there — address, pickup note, your grooming qualification,
Instagram/Facebook links, deposit percentage — you can edit yourself and save.

## Good to know
- **Photos:** any photo you upload is automatically cleaned up (resized, and location data is
  removed so your home address is never hidden inside a photo).
- **Nothing you do can break the site.** Deleting things asks you to confirm first.
- **Emails to customers** (booking confirmations, cancellations) will send automatically once
  your brother connects an email account for the site — until then everything else works fine.
- **Stuck?** Your brother set this up and can help. But the day-to-day above is all yours.
